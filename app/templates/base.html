One platform, three connected roles

Every user signs in from the same door — the system knows who you are and shows you exactly what you need.

Public user · Self-registration

Citizen

Report emergencies, share your live location, and track help as it arrives.

  • Report incidents with photos & GPS
  • Track rescue team ETA & contact
  • Find nearby shelters & relief centers
  • Receive real-time alerts
Responder · Admin-issued credentials

Rescue Team

Accept missions, navigate optimized routes, and report outcomes from the field.

  • View & accept nearby assignments
  • Navigate using optimized routes
  • Update mission & casualty status
  • Request additional resources
EOC staff · Provisioned account only

Administrator

Full oversight of incidents, resources, teams, and disaster analytics.

  • Monitor all incidents in real time
  • Allocate personnel, vehicles & supplies
  • View heatmaps & disaster trend analytics
  • Manage user accounts & permissions

Built-in intelligence, not just reporting

Advanced engines work quietly in the background so responders can focus on the rescue.

Emergency Prioritization Engine

Automatically ranks incidents by severity, people affected, and location risk.

Resource Recommendation Engine

Suggests optimal deployment of teams, vehicles, medical staff and supplies.

Route Optimization

Calculates the fastest, safest paths for rescue teams to reach affected areas.

Disaster Heatmaps

Visualizes incident density and disaster intensity across affected regions.

Real-Time Analytics

Tracks incident frequency, response times, and resource utilization.

Shelter & Relief Locator

Shows nearby shelters, hospitals, and relief centers to affected citizens.
